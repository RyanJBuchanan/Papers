Model Map

State: T = Tstr ⊗ Tobs ⊗ Tenv. The string contributes density, endpoint, and holonomy; the obstacle contributes binding and critical density; the environment contributes RR and NS fields.

Release: the app now supports two interpretations. In elastic mode, elastic energy and NS drive compete against binding and RR stabilization. In rupture mode, low admissibility J(T)<Jcrit can itself trigger a one-time constraint-fracture event, interpreted as winding charge expulsion to the two boundary orbits ∂𝔒 = 𝔬₁ ∪ 𝔬₂. When either event fires, the wound sector projects to unwound and a localized δκ pulse is added to κeff.
